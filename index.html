Alexa Tone Analyzer is an Alexa Skill that is based off of journaling. The user dictates a journal "entry" to Alexa who then calls on IBM Watson to obtain a tonal analysis. The user's entry and tonal analysis along with some metadata is stored on Amazon's DynamoDB.
